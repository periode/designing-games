how do we reconcile both?

A story and a system can be reconciled when behavior is congruent, when it makes sense within a given (real or imagined) situation.

If a strategy game has two kinds of token, a large, heavy character, and a thin, light character, it wouldn't appear to make sense if you could move the heavy character further than the thin light character (as we normally associate heaviness with slowness and lightness with speed).

A good example is theater: in theater, the movement of the actors is always tightly connected to what they say and who they are. No movement is arbitrary, or random, it's always a deliberate decision of the dramaturge.
