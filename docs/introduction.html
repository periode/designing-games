this class

what

an introductory course in game design and development

how

covering fundamental concepts of game design, prototyping games with analog materials, implementing them in unity

why?

games are concentrates of expressive, interactive systems.

play permeates every aspects of our cultures, and can take many forms.

digital games are good technical exercises for media arts.

Making a game is hard, but rewarding. It involves visual design, audio design, programming, storytelling and psychology.

defining games

how do you define play?

  • suspend disbelief, everybody agrees to the fiction, serious but not serious
  • pleasurable but also serious (e.g. russian roulette, lose / lose)
  • non-utilitarian, self-referential, autotelic
  • uncontrolled, freedom within boundaries
  • negotiation of rules
  • balance of effort and reward (practice and goals)

a series of interesting choices (short-term consequence, mid-term and long-term)

a self-inflicted obstacle

defining play

play is not the exclusivity of humans

play exists in its own time/space

play can be interested, or disinterested, but often non-productive

play is uncertain, but can be very serious

play involves rules and make believe
